Best Lasagna...EVER

Description

If you take your time to really be near perfection, it will all be worth it.

We must sometimes treat ourselves after a day of work or if youre just having a bad day, some lasagna will make it much better.

The recipe that I will be presenting comes from allrecipes, which is another website with all kinds of recipes for whatever youd like such as lunch, dinner, desserts, etc., all kinds of meals and sweets.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • One Onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato products used: One can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings:Fresh Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Either store-bought or homemade lasagna
  • Cheeses:Parmesan, Mozzarella, Ricotta
  • One egg

A brief step-by-step on how to make lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mix
  4. Layer lasagna
  5. Cover lasagna using foil and bake
  6. let lasagna rest before serving

How to layer Lasagna:

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Lasagna Noodles
  3. Ricotta Mixture
  4. Mozzarella Slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat layer then top it off with the remaining parmesan

How long to cook Lasagna

The lasagna must take 50 minutes to cook in a oven preheated at 375 degrees fahrenheit. Cover it in foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the last 25. Then let it rest in room temperature for 15 minutes before cutting into it.
