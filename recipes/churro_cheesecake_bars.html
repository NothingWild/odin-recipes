CHURRO CHEESECAKE BARSSSS!!!!

Description

Just when you thought things could not get better... churros and cheesecakse can be put together... to have the best.OF.BOTHHHH. WORLDS. Crazy? Yeah... it is.

Prep Time

15 Minutes

Cook Time

30 Minutes

Total Time

45 Minutes

Ingredients

  • Sugar: Start with 1/2 of a cup of white sugar.
  • CINNAMINNNN
  • Cream Cheese: Two packages of brown sugar and cinnamin spreadable cream cheese
  • One LARGE Egg
  • Two teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • *OPTIONAL* Orange Zest
  • 2 8 ounce refrigerated crescent sheets
  • 1/4 cup salted butter, melted
  • *OPTIONAL* Mexican caramel(Cajeta) for the perfect.finishing.touch.TRUST.

How to make Churro Cheesecake Bars:

  1. Sprinkle the bottom of your prepared pan with sugar and cinnamin.
  2. Place a crescent sheet at the bottom of the pan.
  3. Make the filling, then place it over the crescent sheet.
  4. Top of with remaining crescent sheet, then pour melted butter over the top.
  5. Sprinkle with the remaining sugar and bake until golden brown.

Directions

  1. Gather all Ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly coat a 13x9-inch baking pan with cooking spray.
  2. In a small bowl combine sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle half of the sugar mixture evenly in the bottom of the pan.
  3. Place one crescent sheet in the pan, trimming to fit as needed.
  4. For filling, in a medium bowl beat cream cheese, egg, vanilla, and orange zest with a mixer on medium until smooth.
  5. Spread cream cheese filling evenly over crescent layer in pan.
  6. Gently lay the remaining crescent sheet over the filling.
  7. Pour melted butter over the top.
  8. Sprinkle remaining sugar mixture evenly over top.
  9. Bake until golden brown, about 30 minutes. Cool slightly. Cut into bars. Serve warm or chill and serve within 24 hours. If desired, drizzle with Cajeta just before serving.
