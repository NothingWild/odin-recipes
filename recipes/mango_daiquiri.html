Mango Smoothie basically... just no ice.. and with rum

Description

A nice mango daiquiri (or you can just call it a smoothie honestly) can be a very nice refresher after a long day, especially on a friday or on the weekends. In order to eliminate the use of ice, frozen mango was used instead. You can switch it up whenever you like, but for me, a nice simple mango daiquiri is all I need. It doesn't get any easier than this.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 2 fluid ounces rum
  • 2 fluid ounces freshing squeezed lime juice
  • 2 fluid ounces agave syrup

Directions

Place mango chunks, rum, lime juice, and agave syrup into blender and blend until nice and smooth.
